## Go Conditions

 A condition is used to perform different action based on different conditions.

 A condition can be true or false.

 But Go supports the comparison operators:
 Less than <
 Equal to ==
 Greater than >
 Not equal to !=
 Less than or equal to <=
 Greater than or equal to >=

 And it also supports Logical operators:
 Logical AND &&
 Logical OR ||
 Logical NOT !
